karan ka recipe for rasmalai

ingredients

for saucepan

  • milk
  • sugar
  • bread

cashew

  • kaju
  • badam
  • pista
  • kesar

instructions

  1. boil milk for 20 mins
  2. after 10 mins , add cashew in the milk
  3. keep it on the low flame
  4. meanwhile, cut the bread in round shape
  5. store some cashew in middle of the bread
  6. after 20 mins,get the milk cold
  7. pour the milk on the bread fully deeped
  8. store it in the fridge for one hour
  9. your delicious meal is ready>>>>
